About

This project was developed during the Hack for Change 2013, a event hosted by Google in the National Day of Civic Hacking. The app generate heat map through the variables from Census. So, you can analize where is the potential areas for your needs.
First, you have have to centralize the map on the area that you want to analize. After, you can choose the variables that you want to combine to identify the potential areas. So, you have to click in Generate Map, and wait for results.
